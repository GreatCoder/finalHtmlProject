Food in Italy

Italy is known for its great food culture. It is home to one of the most delicious cuisines in the world. When you visit Italy, tasting the local foods is a must!

Italian Fashion

Many of the world's top fashion designers are located in Italy. Known Italian brands include Giorgio Armani, Roberto Cavalli, Dolce & Gabbana, Gucci, and many others, and their products are sold worldwide.
